The creek bed has faired pretty well over the years.The black pond liner underneath meant that weeds were kept to a minimum, although some weeding was required if leaf litter from overhanging branches built up. As the kids played around the area we did find that rocks “slipped” down from the edges into the centre of the creek bed, so every few months or so I did a little maintenance moving them back out to the edges. The planting around the creek bed looked more natural over time too. Hope that helps! Cath.
