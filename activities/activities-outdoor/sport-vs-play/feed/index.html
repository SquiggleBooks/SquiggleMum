]]> By: Bethe http://www.squigglemum.com/activities/activities-outdoor/sport-vs-play/#comment-4675 Wed, 09 Nov 2011 20:18:16 +0000 http://squigglemum.com/?p=5645#comment-4675 Play is incredibly important and many parents make the mistake of putting kids in sports and thinking it is play time. Play is child-centered and child-directed, and organized sports are anything but. Sports have value, of course, but should come later in childhood. Kids seem to be starting earlier and earlier — before they have learned the fundamental motor skills which come through unstructured play but also through structured, developmentally appropriate movement experiences & play activities guided by a teacher, caregiver or parent. These experiences will form the basis for everything — sports, dance and physical recreation pursuits into adulthood.

For great movement focused activities, visit Head Start Body Start: http://www.aahperd.org/headstartbodystart/activityresources/
