Loveliness

July 10, 2009FaithSquiggleMum

I was so touched to receive this lovely blog award from Sarah at Faith Hope & A Whole Lotta Love.

Passing it on to 15 other bloggers is completely beyond me, but here are five Aussie Christian mummy-bloggers who I’d like to encourage.  These blogs are not necessarily high-traffic sites, but they are lovely simply because the women behind them are lovely.  Stop by and say hi.

Moments for Mum

For I Know The Plans I Have For You

Nearly Super Mum

A-D Libbing

Don’t Die Wondering

Ladies, up to you whether you’d like to accept the award and pass it on, or just smile and feel loved. If you want to play along the rules are:  Accept the award, post it on your blog together with the name of the person who has granted the award and his or her blog link.  Pass the award to 15 (!!) other blogs (I came up with 5) that you have discovered. Remember to contact the bloggers to let them know they have been chosen for this award.

With blessings, Cath.
