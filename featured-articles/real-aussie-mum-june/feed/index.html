]]> By: Janelle http://www.squigglemum.com/featured-articles/real-aussie-mum-june/#comment-303 Tue, 09 Jun 2009 12:27:30 +0000 http://squigglemum.com/?p=811#comment-303 Here’s the recipe for all you spaghetti lovers –
Ingredients: 1kg beef mince, 1 diced onion, 1 tsp minced garlic, 1 tsp dried Italian herbs, 1 large grated carrot, 1 large grated zucchini, 2 cups finely chopped mushrooms, 1/4 cup tomato paste, 2 cans diced tomatoes, 1 cup beef stock, 1 500g bottle pasta sauce, 1/4 cup grated cheese.
Method: brown the mince, onion and garlic. Add the tomato paste, herbs and other veggies and cook for about 3-5 minutes until veggies are soft & golden. Add the 2 cans of tomatoes and beef stock and simmer uncovered for about 20 minutes until reduced. Add the pasta sauce about 2 minutes before serving to make a nice consistency. Serve with spaghetti and sprinkle with cheese! This feeds my family of 5 for two meals. Try making a lasagne with the leftovers for something different!
