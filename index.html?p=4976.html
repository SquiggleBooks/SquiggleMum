Seeds for the Future Giveaway

March 29, 2011Outdoor, Win!SquiggleMum

My daughter has only just embarked on her 13yr school journey – and already I am over chocolate drive fundraisers!  I am always interested to hear about unique fundraising ideas, and this has to be one of the best I’ve seen.  Educational? Check. Australian? Check. Profitable? Check. Ethical? Check.

If that sounds like your kind of fundraiser too, you have to check out Seeds for the Future.  It’s a simple fundraising concept: buy a seed packet for $1 and sell it for $2.  That means a whopping 50% profit for your school, kindy, playgroup, MOPS group, whatever.  (Be aware though that you will have to outlay for the seed packets first.)  Seeds come in 25pkt boxes, and contain either a warm or cool selection of herbs and vegetables depending on the season and your climate zone.  There are even tips for successful growing on the site too!  What a fanstastic way to encourage families to get gardening together, while fundraising for your community group.  Contact Seeds for the Future if you’d like a free fundraising kit and seed sample pack.


Fancy a little seed raising with the kids?  Thanks to my generous friends at Seeds for the Future I have TEN mixed selection packs to give away to TEN SquiggleMum readers. Yay!! To enter, leave a comment sharing your gardening-with-kids experience below.  Are you an avid grower of veggies in the backyard?  Are you a sometimes-gardener with a small patch of herbs?  Or have you never-ever-ever tried gardening with your children?

Usual rules apply: comp closes Friday 1/4/11 6pm Qld time, ten winners selected at random, one entry per person, Aussie residents only please.

*   *   *

This comp has now closed. Congratulations:

Natalie, Johanna, Nicole, Brooke, Janet, Amy Keep, Samantha Savva, Ros, Jenny and Kathryn C!

Happy gardening
