Moments for Mum Giveaway!

September 4, 2009Faith, Featured ArticlesSquiggleMum

WINNER ANNOUNCED!

Congratulations to Gail who has won a signed copy of Narelle’s devotional book Moments for Mum.  It was quite tricky to count the entries for this comp, but we had 21 entries and the randomly chosen winning number was 19.  Thanks to those who are now following this blog, or Narelle’s blog.  Thanks also to everyone who shared Bible verses – I didn’t realise what an encouragement this would be to me!


“Busy” doesn’t begin to describe life as a mum.  It’s a constant battle of competing priorities for my time, and my own needs rarely rate a mention.  If I have ten minutes while the kids are busy with my hubby, chances are I’m jumping into the shower!  I sometimes get to the end of the day and realise I haven’t spent any time with God at all.  It should be one of my highest ranking priorities, but I’ll be the first to admit that I really struggle to set aside time to pray and read my Bible.

I know that using a devotional book is a helpful strategy for me, and this week a lovely package arrived in my letterbox.  I purchased Moments for Mum – 61 Daily Devotions written by fellow Aussie blogger Narelle Nettelbeck, and she sent me an extra copy for me to give away to a SquiggleMum reader!  She even signed it!!  (Thanks Narelle.)

So far Moments for Mum has been a real encouragement to me.  Each devotion is only one or two pages long, contains a bible verse to consider and is relevant to the crazy stage of life I’m in as a mother.  Narelle writes openly and honestly about her joys and struggles, and her book is very easy to read.  I’m just reading one devotion each day, when I put both kids into their rooms for quiet time after lunch.  Before I race around doing whatever housework is begging to be done, before I open the emails demanding my attention, before I do anything (other than go to the loo), I’m going to have a cup of coffee and open Moments for Mum.  I know that even a little bit of time spent with God has a big impact on my day.

Would you like to join me?  There are a few ways you can enter to win your own copy of Moments for Mum.  You can either:

  • share your favourite Bible verse in the comments below
  • pop over to Narelle’s blog, Moments for Mum, and become a follower (leave a comment here to let me know)
  • subscribe to this blog via email or RSS (leave a comment here to let me know)

And yes, you can do all three and enter three times if you want to (but please leave three separate comments)!  The comp is open to Aussies only, and the winner will be drawn randomly at 6pm next Friday 11th September.

PS – I know this giveaway won’t interest all of my readers, and that’s cool.
