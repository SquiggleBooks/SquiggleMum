Ukulele Baby: Wiggle-Squiggle Giveaway

March 1, 2011Win!entertainment, musicSquiggleMum

The ukulele is such a fun instrument.  Its small size makes it ideal for young kids to handle, they are inexpensive (usually around $25) and come in a range of colours.  Of course, the ukulele is more than just a “toy guitar” as it is also a serious instrument in its own right.

Ukulele Baby! is the latest offering from Aussie children’s superstars The Wiggles, and explores the sound of the ukulele and the feel of music from the caribbean.  The Wiggles have set sail on a new adventure with the sweet sound of strumming and palm trees in the distance. Set on an island near Wiggle Bay, The Wiggles have launched twenty three new songs including soon-to-be-classics like You Can Play the Ukulele and Hula, Hula Baby. The album also features a couple of special guests. The highlight for me is the hilarious revival of a 70’s kids song, The Good Ship Fabulous Flea! with Rolf Harris.

Thanks to my wiggly friends at Roadshow I have FOUR copies of Ukulele Baby! to give away to SquiggleMum readers. To enter, leave a comment sharing who your favourite Wiggle (or Wiggles character) is. Aussie residents only please. One entry per person. Comp closes Sunday 6/3/11 6pm Qld time. Winners chosen at random.

You can also pick up a copy of Ukulele Baby! at ABC Stores or online Price: $14.95

*I received a copy of Ukulele Baby! courtesy of Roadshow.
